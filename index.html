Audience in the Loop

Viewer Feedback-Driven Content Creation in Micro-drama Production

Gengchen Cao*¹  ·  Tianke He*²  ·  Yixuan Liu³  ·  RAY LC³⁺
¹Tsinghua University  |  ²Sichuan University of Media and Communication  |  ³City University of Hong Kong

Abstract

The shift toward byte-sized narrative content has transformed how stories are told. In platforms like Douyin and Kuaishou, micro-dramas thrive on fast-paced action and emotional cliffs.

Our study of 28 professional scriptwriters reveals a new paradigm: Audience-Directed Workflows. Writers now operate as multi-role creators, weaving real-time feedback—comments, reposts, and memes—directly into the iterative fabric of the story.

Iterative Adaptation

Moving from static scripts to dynamic storylines that evolve with real-time feedback.

Collaborative Creation

Revealing audience interaction as a primary driver in social media paradigms.

Key Insights

We identified two emerging styles in the micro-drama ecosystem:

  • AI-Generated Micro-dramas: Efficiency-driven production pipelines.
  • Audience-Responsive Micro-dramas: Highly interactive, community-led narratives.
